About our classes

A3JM runs additional voluntary tuition classes for students who want to strengthen their understanding outside regular school hours. Our weekend slots are designed so that weekdays stay free for school and rest.

  • Small groups for better attention
  • Saturday & Sunday only
  • Two sessions per day: 9 AM – 12 PM and 3 PM – 6 PM
  • Multiple subjects and grades

Class schedule
